RFRM

Staying informed about important social and political issues is no longer an option, it’s the minimum. RFRM is here to ease the work. RFRM is an educational platform that allows users to stay up to date with current events through podcast, news article, or ebook.

Role

UX/UI Designer

Timeline

1.5 months

Process

Through a competitor analysis, I found the market for apps that provided this level of coverage over the varying issues plaguing the country to be unsaturated and dismissed.

From there, I set out with user flow diagrams to create low- and mid-fidelity wireframes to understand the user’s thinking and journey.

Feeling confident with the usability, I then created high-fidelity wireframes and recruited members of the design community to provide valuable feedback before finalizing the design and creating a function, final user interface prototype.
