About Me

My Process

Throughout my experiences in studio arts, scenic design, I am continually fascinated by the connection between intriguing visuals and human interaction. This interest motivates my UX/U designs by focusing on research, testing, and feedback to understand the user and their needs. With an empathetic mindset, I create clean and clear, human-centered designs.

My Background

My journey in visual design began in college where I studied painting, drawing, and sculpture. I created work that, though stemmed from personal circumstances, sought to evoke emotions and discussions from viewers.

Following graduation, I landed a job building and styling thematic environments.These sets were experiences that were both transportive and functional, allowing the audiences to move and interact with props.

With the interest of furthering my education, I set out on a self-taught training in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The more I learned about these languages, I decided to marry my passion for design and my growing interest in coding and enrolled in the UI Design program at CareerFoundry.
